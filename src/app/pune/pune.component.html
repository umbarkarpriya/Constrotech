Majestique Landmarks

Majestique was initially established as a textiles industry during the year 1975 by Dwarkadas Maheshwari. The company soon extended its horizons on to multiple domains, one of them being real estate. The real estate wing of Majestique commenced operations in the year 2007 under the leadership of Shyamkumar Maheshwari, son of Dwarkadas Maheshwari. In today’s date, Majestique Landmarks is one of the most premium brands in the sector of real estate.

Pristine Properties

Pristine Properties has emerged as a reputed, well-known real estate developer in India. It commenced operations in the year 1988 and ever since it has initiated multiple projects in the most prime locations of Pune. Without compromising style or creativity, Pristine Properties has always focused on the aspect of Vastu Shastra. Since the company is a strong supporter of all things eco-friendly, it has equipped most of its housing projects with facilities such as rainwater harvesting, vermicomposting, and water recycling.
